Starting from 1st October 2026, customers holding Eterna, Eterna FD, Tiara or Varunah Premium credit 
card must spend ₹75,000 or more in the previous calendar quarter to qualify for unlimited domestic 
lounge access. 
 
For example: If you have spent ₹75,000 or more between July–September 2026 then you will be eligible 
for unlimited domestic lounge access between October–December 2026. This cycle will continue for 
future quarters i. e. lounge eligibility for given calendar quarter will be decided basis spends in preceding 
calendar quarter.
