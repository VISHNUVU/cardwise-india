What similar Indian sites do

  • BankBazaar starts with employment type, then checks eligibility and routes toward application offers.
  • Paisabazaar combines editorial “top card” lists, card-level comparison, a mobile-number-led pre-approved-offer flow, fees, ratings and category tags.
  • CardExpert is more editorial and enthusiast-led, emphasizing detailed reviews, reward optimization and community knowledge.
  • Common opportunity: show a no-login value model before lead capture; disclose ranking logic, coverage and commercial relationships; separate “good fit” from “likely eligible.”
